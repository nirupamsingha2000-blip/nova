PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Who we are

Project NOVA is the website of Nirupam Science Edge, a science coaching institute based in Shillong, Meghalaya. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at info@nse.com.

Information we collect

Contact / demo booking form: student name, phone number, program of interest, and any message you write.
Newsletter form: your email address.
AI assistant chat widget: whatever text you type into the chat, sent to our server solely to generate a reply.

How we use it

To respond to enrollment enquiries, follow up about demo classes, and — only if you opt in via the newsletter form — send occasional updates about demo dates and exam tips. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Third-party processors

Formspree processes submissions from our contact and newsletter forms and delivers them to our inbox.
Google Maps is embedded on our Contact page and may set its own cookies per Google's own privacy policy.
Anthropic receives the text you send our AI assistant solely to generate a reply — we do not store your chat history beyond your current browser session.

Student & Teacher Portal

The Student Portal and Teacher Portal pages on this site are front-end demo/preview dashboards only. The login credentials shown are placeholders — no real student accounts, passwords, or academic data are created, transmitted, or stored through them.

Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage (not cookies) to remember your light/dark theme preference and whether you've already seen our intro animation. This information stays in your browser and is never transmitted to us.

Children's privacy

Many of our students are minors. We ask that demo class bookings and enquiries involving a student be made with a parent or guardian's involvement.

Data retention & security

We keep enquiry details only as long as needed to respond to you and follow up about enrollment, and we take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us.

Your rights

You can ask us to correct or delete your information at any time by emailing info@nse.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. Changes take effect as soon as they're posted here.
